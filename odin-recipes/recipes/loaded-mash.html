Ultimate Loaded Mashed Potatoes

Description

These loaded mashed potatoes are one of my most popular dishes that I make aroun the crib. Perfect for football sundays, small "get-togethers" or for a pre-workout carb load-up--this recipe is simply to die for.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Sour Cream
  • Butter
  • Bacon
  • Chives
  • Cheese
  • Scallions

Steps

  1. Mash dem 'taters': Cut then boil some potatoes. Once soft enough to poke a fork through, remove the bioled potatoes and mash them with a potato masher.
  2. Mix, mix, mix Next, add sour cream and butter to the mashed potatoes. Mix well until they take on a buttery consistency.
  3. Top it off: Heat some cheese atop the mix. Pnce melted, add the bacon, chives, and scallions to the top
  4. Indulge: Stuff your face in the gooey goodness of potatoes. You've earned it, champ!
